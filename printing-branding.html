Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you do the actual physical printing too?

A: We specialize primarily in print design layouts, setting up precise files ready for direct machinery execution. We can also assist you in coordinating with premium local offset print houses seamlessly.

Q: What color profiles do you use for layouts?

A: All print layouts are strictly processed using standard CMYK color charts at full 300 DPI resolution, preventing any unexpected color changes during production.
